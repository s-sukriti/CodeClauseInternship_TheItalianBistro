Our Menu

Explore our delicious menu featuring a wide variety of authentic Italian dishes

Appetizers

---------------------

Bruschetta al Pomodoro

Toasted bread topped with diced tomatoes, garlic, basil, and olive oil.

Caprese Salad

Slices of fresh mozzarella, tomatoes, and basil, drizzled with balsamic glaze.

Calamari Fritti

Crispy fried calamari served with marinara sauce for dipping.

Arancini

Fried risotto balls stuffed with mozzarella cheese and served with marinara sauce.

Garlic Bread

Crusty Italian bread toasted with garlic butter.

Antipasto Platter

Assortment of cured meats, cheeses, olives, and roasted vegetables.

Main Courses

-------------------------

Spaghetti Bolognese

Classic Italian pasta dish with a rich beef and tomato sauce.

Spaghetti Carbonara

Classic Roman pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper.

Risotto ai Funghi

Creamy risotto cooked with wild mushrooms, Parmesan cheese, and white wine.

Lasagna

Layers of pasta sheets, Bolognese sauce, bechamel, and melted cheese.

Pizza Margherita

Traditional Italian pizza topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil.

Osso Buco

Braised veal shanks served with a flavorful gremolata.

Desserts

----------------

Tiramisu

Classic Italian dessert made with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and mascarpone cheese, dusted with cocoa powder.

Cannoli

Crispy pastry shells filled with sweetened ricotta cheese and chocolate chips, garnished with powdered sugar.

Gelato

Smooth and creamy Italian ice cream available in a variety of flavors such as chocolate, vanilla, and pistachio.

Drinks

-------------

Espresso

Strong black coffee brewed by forcing hot water under pressure through finely-ground coffee beans.

Cappuccino

Espresso combined with steamed milk and topped with a frothy milk foam, often dusted with cocoa powder or cinnamon.

Negroni

Classic Italian cocktail made with equal parts gin, vermouth rosso, and Campari, served over ice and garnished with an orange twist.

Aperol Spritz

Refreshing cocktail made with Aperol, Prosecco, and a splash of soda water, served over ice with an orange slice.

Italian Soda

Carbonated water mixed with flavored syrup, often served with a splash of cream.

Limoncello

Italian lemon liqueur made from the zest of lemons, often served chilled as a digestif.
